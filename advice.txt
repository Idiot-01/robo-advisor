Based on the time-series provided, which tracks the **mNAV (Market Net Asset Value)** ratio for MicroStrategy (MSTR) from April 2025 through April 2026, here is the analysis:

### 1. What the current mNAV value signifies
The current value (as of April 16, 2026) is **0.8493**.

In the context of MicroStrategy, mNAV represents the ratio between the company’s Market Capitalization and the total value of the Bitcoin (BTC) it holds on its balance sheet.
*   **A value of 1.0** means the market is pricing MSTR exactly at the value of its Bitcoin holdings (Parity).
*   **A value > 1.0** means MSTR is trading at a "premium" (investors are paying extra for the company’s software business, its ability to use leverage, or its yield-generating strategies).
*   **A value < 1.0** means MSTR is trading at a "discount." 

**Significance:** At **0.8493**, the market is currently pricing MicroStrategy at a **~15% discount** to its Bitcoin holdings. This signifies that the "MSTR Premium" has entirely evaporated. Investors are essentially able to buy Bitcoin via MSTR shares for 85 cents on the dollar, while the company’s underlying software business is effectively being valued at less than zero.
