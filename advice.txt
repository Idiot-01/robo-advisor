Based on the time-series data provided (which covers April 2025 through April 2026), here is an analysis of MicroStrategy’s (MSTR) mNAV (Market-to-Net Asset Value) ratio.

The latest value is **0.8208** (as of April 15, 2026).

*   **Trading at a Discount:** An mNAV of 1.0 means the market values MSTR exactly at the value of its Bitcoin holdings. A value of **0.82** signifies that MicroStrategy is trading at a **18% discount** to its underlying Bitcoin assets.
*   **Market Sentiment:** This indicates extreme "under-valuation" or high "fear." Historically, MSTR has often traded at a premium (1.5x to 2.5x). Trading below 1.0 suggests the market is either skeptical of the company’s ability to service its debt or is currently in a period of extreme bearishness where investors prefer holding BTC directly rather than the stock.
