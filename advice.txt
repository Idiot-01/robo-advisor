Based on the time-series provided for MicroStrategy (MSTR), here is an analysis of the **mNAV** (Market value to Net Asset Value ratio). 

For context, mNAV measures the price of the stock relative to the value of its Bitcoin holdings. 
*   **mNAV > 1.0:** The stock is trading at a **premium** (investors are paying more for the stock than the BTC is worth).
*   **mNAV < 1.0:** The stock is trading at a **discount** (investors are buying BTC via the stock for less than the spot price).

---

### 1. What the current mNAV value signifies
The most recent value (April 25, 2026) is **0.9432**.

*   **Trading at a Discount:** This signifies that MSTR is currently trading at approximately a **5.7% discount** to its Net Asset Value. 
*   **Implied Valuation:** Essentially, the market is valuing the entire company (including its software business and its ability to leverage debt to buy more BTC) at less than the market value of the Bitcoin it currently holds on its balance sheet.
*   **Sentiment:** This usually indicates a period of local "capitulation" or extreme skepticism toward the stock's ability to maintain its historical premium.

*   **Massive De-rating:** One year ago (April 2025), MSTR was trading at a massive premium of **1.83 to 2.02**. Investors were paying double the price of the underlying Bitcoin to own the stock.

*   **Bottoming out:** For the first few months of 2026, the mNAV has largely fluctuated between **0.77 and 0.95**, suggesting that the "Premium Era" of 2025 has been completely wiped out.

#### The Past Month (Late March 2026 – April 25, 2026)
