### 1. What the current mNAV value signifies
The current mNAV value (as of April 18, 2026) is **0.9557**.

In the context of MSTR, mNAV is typically the ratio of the company's Market Capitalization to the value of its Bitcoin holdings.
*   **A value of 1.0** means the market is pricing MSTR exactly at the value of its Bitcoin (the "Sats-per-share" parity).
*   **A value > 1.0** means the stock is trading at a **premium**. Investors are paying extra for the company’s software business, its ability to use leverage to buy more BTC, or its tax-loss carryforwards.
*   **A value < 1.0** means the stock is trading at a **discount**. You are effectively buying Bitcoin for less than its market price through the equity.

**Significance:** At **0.9557**, MSTR is currently trading at a **slight discount (~4.4%)** to its underlying Bitcoin holdings. This suggests the "hype premium" that existed a year ago has completely evaporated, and the stock is currently undervalued relative to the spot price of the Bitcoin it owns.
